  • Collection

    The first step involves gathering plastic waste from various sources, such as households and industries.

  • Sorting and Categorization

    The collected plastic is sorted by type and color to ensure compatibility, as mixing different plastics can reduce the quality of the final recycled material.

  • Washing

    The sorted plastics are washed to remove contaminants like dirt, labels, and food residue.

  • Shredding

    The clean plastic is then shredded into smaller pieces, often called flakes, which makes it easier to process.

  • Melting and Pelletizing

    The flakes are heated and melted into a liquid, which is then extruded into filaments and cut into small pellets or granules.

  • Manufacturing

    These recycled plastic pellets are used to create new products, such as bottles, packaging, or other plastic items.
